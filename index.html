ROT-13 Encryptor & Decryptor

Rot13 or Rot-13 (short for rotate 13) is a simple letter substitution encryption scheme. It works by replacing the current english letters in a message with those that are 13 positions ahead in the alphabet. For example, letter a is replaced by n,b,by,o,c,by,p,etc.Numbers and punctuation are not encoded.
